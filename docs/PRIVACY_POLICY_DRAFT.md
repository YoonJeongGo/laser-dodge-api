# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-07

Laser Dodge is provided by 박강열.

## Data We Collect

When you use online ranking or social login, we may collect:

- Nickname
- Login provider identifier from Google, Naver, or Facebook
- Game scores, survival time, point score, rank, and timestamps
- Basic profile name supplied by the login provider

We do not intentionally collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or sensitive personal data.

## How We Use Data

We use data to:

- Create and manage your game profile
- Show leaderboards and rankings
- Prevent score abuse and maintain fair play
- Diagnose service errors

## Sharing

We use third-party login providers only for authentication. We do not sell personal data.

Your nickname and score may be shown to other players in the leaderboard.

## Security

Online requests are intended to be transmitted over HTTPS in production. Server secrets are stored on the backend and are not intentionally exposed in the app.

## Retention and Deletion

Leaderboard records are retained while the online ranking service is operated. To request deletion of your account or leaderboard data, contact:
